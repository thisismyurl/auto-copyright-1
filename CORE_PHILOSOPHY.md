# CORE_PHILOSOPHY.md

## 1. Helpful Neighbor Tone
We aim to contribute positively to our community and collaborate closely with other repositories, promoting a culture of sharing and support. We believe in fostering an environment where ideas can flourish without fear of infringement or competition. By maintaining a helpful neighbor tone, we encourage constructive feedback, respectful dialogue, and mutual assistance among all contributors.

## 2. Safe by Default
We prioritize the security and integrity of our codebase and user data. Our default settings are designed to protect against potential vulnerabilities and unintended exposures. This includes implementing robust encryption methods, regular security audits, and adhering to best practices in software development to ensure that safety is a fundamental aspect of our work.

## 3. Murphy's Law (Resilience)
In the spirit of resilience, we accept that challenges are inevitable. We view setbacks as opportunities for learning and growth rather than insurmountable obstacles. By embracing Murphy’s Law, we motivate ourselves to be adaptable and proactive in overcoming difficulties. This attitude not only enhances our problem-solving skills but also builds a robust and resilient community that can face any challenge head-on.
